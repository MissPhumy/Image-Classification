About Image Classification

Image classification is a crucial task in computer vision where an algorithm is used to categorize an image into a particular class or label. Various models, are employed to achieve high accuracy in this task.

Types of Models

Different models like CNNs, ResNet, VGG, and EfficientNet are commonly used in image classification. Each model has its own strengths and is used depending on the specific requirements of the task.

EfficientNet Models

EfficientNet is a family of models that balance accuracy and efficiency. They use a compound scaling method to scale up models effectively, providing high accuracy with fewer computational resources.
